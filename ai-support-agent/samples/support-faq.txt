1. ABOUT SUPPORTDESK
================================================================================

SupportDesk is an AI-powered customer support platform for small and medium
businesses. Businesses upload their help documents, embed a chat widget on
their website, and customers get instant answers 24/7.

Plans:
- Starter: $29/month — 1,000 AI conversations, 1 agent seat
- Pro: $79/month — 5,000 AI conversations, 5 agent seats
- Enterprise: Custom pricing — unlimited conversations, SSO

All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

================================================================================
2. PASSWORD RESET
================================================================================

1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot password?"
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (valid for 1 hour)
4. Create a new password (minimum 8 characters, at least one number)

If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, check spam or contact
support@supportdesk.io.

================================================================================
3. RETURNS & REFUNDS
================================================================================

Return window: 30 days from delivery for unused items in original packaging.

Non-returnable: Digital downloads, personalized products, perishable goods.

How to start a return:
1. Email returns@yourstore.com with your order number
2. Receive a return authorization (RA) number within 2 business days
3. Ship the item back with the RA number on the package
4. Refund processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the item
